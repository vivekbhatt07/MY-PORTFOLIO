Game Experience

In my childhood, I generally play games on my computer that time mobile games were not that much popular and exciting to play. Also, we don't possess mobile phones that time surely. In PC, I have played many games like Max Payne, Total Overdose, Prince of Persia, GTA vice city etc. This list is very huge.

But as I reach my adulthood, my pc became old with low specification unable to run high-end games which became a major reason for me to stop playing games on my PC. After sometime, pubg was launched in India. I got excited about it and wanted to play it. I played it for many years and enjoyed it a lot with friends. I think It created a better bonding between me and my friends to connect even if we are far away from each other.

Everthing was fine until it got banned and everyone was soo sad regarding it. But it came again after a long period of altleast 1.5 year rebranded as "BGMI".
