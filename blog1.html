My Coding Journey

Since my B.com graduation, I had been in a dilemma about what to do after graduation as there were many options to pursue to decide my career journey. So, I was on track of my life where I needed to choose the best among the available alternatives to my career path.

Firstly, I made up my mind to pursue goverment job as it seems secure. But the cost of getting it is to start from zero and study consisitantly. And also in preparing for any government exams there comes a fear what if we did'nt pass then at the end we remain empty handed. I don't mind in doing hardwork in anything but the problem with me is that I am a slow learner and takes time to grasp things.

While there were no vacancy for any government jobs, my parents send me to Indor city, where my brother works at a company. There my brother work as a Front End Developer so he also get me there just to learn not on internship. As he told me that Internships will not be beneficial for me as I don't know the basics of programming languages .So firstly, I had to learn it by myself and if I face any difficulties then I can ask anybody in the office.

The people in the office were very supportive, they helped me in every aspect possible. I started learning from Tanay Pratap videos from neog camp which I had started watching on November, 2021, so that I will get admission in neog.camp. But the topics covered seems to go over my head and I got frustrated as it was mentioned beginner but it seems tough. Due to which I did'nt get admission that year as I was unable to complete the projects mentioned in his level 0 course.
